SAN DIEGO
COMIC-CON

Live Data App Concept & Design

Thousands of Comic-Con attendees cosplay, or dress up in costume, during the event. These cosplayers want to connect with people of similar interests and meet other cosplayers with the same interests.

However, with the overwhelming size of this convention, it can be hard if not impossible to find and connect with other people who are also cosplaying within your fandom.

I created a service that makes finding and connecting with other cosplayers with simliar interests as you fun and easy. In doing so, it will keep con-goers happy, and help reinforce the community aspect of the convention.

This app is paired with iBeacon technology to provide more accurate locations from within the convention center.
