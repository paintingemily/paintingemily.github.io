SAN DIEGO
COMIC-CON

Website & Branding Redesign

San Diego Comic Con is a comic book and pop culture convention held annually in downtown San Diego. The convention attracts over 130,000 visitors, with demand growing exponentially every year.

However, their current website is hard to navigate, aesthetically unpleasing, and not engaging for users.

I redesigned the website to improve user flow and create a more pleasing, engaging, and helpful interface for potential attendees. Bold colors and large imagery were used to capture the intensity and excitement surrounding the convention.
