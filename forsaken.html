Forsaken Book Series

Responsive Website Design

The Forsaken is a teen book series by Lisa Stasse set in a not too distant dystopian future where teens who test positive for capacity for violence are sent to a desolate island.

I created a responsive website design to mimic the feel and tone of the book, taking creative inspiration from the book covers (dystopian imagery with futuristic interface)..

On the desktop version, we wanted to keep the website engaging and not text heavy, favoring video backgrounds to help immerse the user into the Forsaken universe. On the mobile integration, we made the images static, but kept the text limited but within the brand considerations.
